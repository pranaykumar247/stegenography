Steganography is an ancient art of hiding secret messages within seemingly innocent data, allowing individuals to communicate covertly without arousing suspicion. It's a digital form of "hidden in plain sight."

This tool empowers you to encode a secret message or information into an image using steganography techniques. Your message becomes a hidden treasure, waiting to be uncovered.

Note: Decoding text from an image involves extracting the concealed message from the image's data. This process is akin to revealing a hidden gem within a seemingly ordinary stone, and it may subtly impact the image's visual appearance.

Always keep a backup of the original image, as the decoding process may introduce subtle alterations to the image's characteristics.
